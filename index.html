Rings Puzzle:ASMR

2021-03-30


Rings Puzzle is a simple and colorful puzzle game.
Match the same color Rings on the row.

FEATURES:

  • Designed for tablets & phones and more devices.
  • Fast start up and play easily.
  • 100% free game to play.
  • Complete puzzling rings challenge and get the score as high as you can.

HOW TO PLAY:

  • Simply drag color rings and put them into the grid.
  • Arrange and stack the color rings on the vertically, horizontally and diagonal lines.
  • Match the color ring.

Mahjong Merge:ASMR

2021-03-18


Mahjong Merge is a simple and fun number merge puzzle. The goal of the game is to get bigger point of mahjong.
Challenge your brain.

HOW TO PLAY:

  1. Click on the mahjong to add 1 point to it, and merge mahjongs - eliminate it with the same point around it and then plus 1
  2. Match at least 3 same mahjongs to merge

WHAT IS FUN:

  • Multiple interesting theme and tiles
  • Simple math rules. Tap and merge!
  • Merge mahjongs to reach high score!. What is the highest score you can get?
